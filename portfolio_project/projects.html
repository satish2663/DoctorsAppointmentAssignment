Project 1: Prediction of Hepatitis Disease

Project Overview:

Developed a model to enhance hepatitis illness prediction accuracy using Support Vector Machine (SVM), ensuring robust performance in regression and classification tasks.

Roles and Responsibilities:

Executed Stratify K Fold Cross Validation for balanced dataset distribution, developed a streamlined model deployment process, conducted performance evaluations, and collaborated with medical professionals to integrate real-time data sources.

Technologies Used:

Python, SVM, Stratify K Fold Cross Validation, Data Integration, Model Deployment.

Challenges and Solutions:

Ensured balanced dataset distribution through Stratify K Fold Cross Validation, reduced deployment time and increased operational efficiency with a streamlined process, and improved predictive accuracy through collaboration with medical professionals and real-time data integration.

Results and Impact:

Achieved a 20% reduction in false positives and false negatives, increased operational efficiency, improved predictive accuracy by 25%, and maintained a minimum precision and recall within the first six months.
